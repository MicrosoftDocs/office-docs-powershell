# New-CsTeamsUpdateManagementPolicy

## SYNOPSIS
Creates a new Teams Update Management policy.

## SYNTAX

```
New-CsTeamsUpdateManagementPolicy [-UpdateDayOfWeek <Object>] [-Description <String>] [-UpdateTime <Object>]
 [-WhatIf] [-UpdateTimeOfDay <Object>] [-Confirm] [-AllowManagedUpdates <Boolean>] [[-Identity] <Object>]
 [-AllowPreview <Boolean>] [-Tenant <Object>] [-InMemory] [-Force] [-AsJob]
```

## DESCRIPTION
Creates a new Teams Update Management policy.

## EXAMPLES

### Example 1
```powershell
PS C:\> New-CsTeamsUpdateManagementPolicy -Identity AllowsPreviews -AllowPreview $true
```

Creates a new Teams Update Management policy that allows showing preview features.
